Our 14-Step Painting Process

Our meticulous process ensures high-quality results for both interior and exterior painting projects.

  • Pressure Wash: We’ll pressure wash the exterior of the house from top to bottom to remove all dirt, debris, and cobwebs thoroughly. (For homes built after 1978)
  • Scrape and Sand Loose Paint: We scrape, sand, and carefully remove flaking paint from both interior and exterior surfaces.
  • Reset Loose Nails: We’ll set appropriately loose nails on siding, trim, and eaves unless otherwise instructed.
  • Prime Bare Areas: We properly prime bare areas, including metal gutters and downspouts with galvanized and rust-inhibitive primers, and stucco and wood surfaces with suitable primers.
  • Caulk Windows and Door Frames: We caulk windows and door frames to enhance the paint appearance and help insulate windows. This does not apply to stucco home windows. Caulking may expand or shrink due to weather or temperature.
  • Patch Cracks In Stucco: We patch cracks in stucco as needed.
  • Remove and Replace Fixtures: We take off light fixtures, address numbers, house racks, etc., prior to painting and carefully replace them upon completion.
  • Patch Cracked Boards: We patch cracks in fascia, siding, trim boards, etc., as needed for protection.
  • Repair Leaky Gutters: We seal and patch leaky gutters as needed. This is a non-guaranteed cosmetic fix.
  • Protect Windows And Gardens: We fully cover all windows and gardens prior to painting to avoid any damage.
  • Drips, Sags, And Runs: We professionally apply paint with the correct thickness to ensure no drips, sags, or runs.
  • Apply Major Paint Brand: We use major brands such as Sherwin Williams and Kelly Moore for high-quality results.
  • Spotless Clean Up: We clean all walkways, sidewalks, and unpainted areas to satisfaction.
